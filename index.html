My Schedule

By: Angela Wang

Hello! My name is Angela Wang, I’m currently a Sophomore at Staten Island Technical Highschool. I’m 14 years old, turning 15 on October 17, 2007. My favorite colors are purple and pink. I enjoy drawing, as well as coding, and took realistic still-life art classes in middle school.

I’m currently thinking about joining the stage crew for SING, as well as getting involved with more things in school such as more clubs and volunteering for things like the freshman social. My schedule is listed below, along with my thoughts on my experience so far, feel free to talk to me if you have any of the same classes as me!

AP Computer Science Principles

(Micheal Whalen)

Mr, Whalen has a sense of humor that you rarely see in teachers, with basically no homework at all, unless you're behind on your projects in school. That is to say, I’ve been lagging a bit behind with my projects because I’m not used to using a computer with all its controls and whatnot. But in the end, I do enjoy learning about JavaScript/coding for designing websites and such. (N)
